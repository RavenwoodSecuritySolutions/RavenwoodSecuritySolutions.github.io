Client Agreement

Before you can claim protection under the Ravenwood Security Solutions Client Umbrella, you must agree to all sections of this agreement.

Section 1 - Rules Of Engagement

By agreeing to this section of the Client Agreement, you agree to respect and follow the Member Rules Of Engagement, specified in our Member Agreement.

Section 2 - Exclusivity Of Combat

While bound under this section of the Client Agreement, you understand and accept that if you attack another Client of Ravenwood Security Solutions, we will retaliate as we are obligated to. You also understand that should you choose to attack another Client, you will be permanently removed as a Client of Ravenwood.

This means your protection under the Client Umbrella will be null and void, and you will be removed as a Client in the Discord Server.
You will not be eligible for protection from Ravenwood Security Solutions and you will not be allowed within the RSS compound.

Section 3 - Client Umbrella Protections

After agreeing to the Client Agreement, you will fall under the Ravenwood Client Umbrella, which affords you protections and benefits provided by Ravenwood and it's Members.
These include, but are not limited to:

  • Raid protection, provided by Ravenwood Charlie Core.
  • If applicable and accepted, penetration testing and security consultation provided by Ravenwood Charlie Core.
  • If applicable and accepted, counterraiding and counterraid assistance provided by Ravenwood Lambda Core.

You may choose to opt out of one or more of these protections during your application process.
Please note that opting back into these protections will be accepted at the sole discretion of individual you negotiated your contract with, and is not guaranteed.
