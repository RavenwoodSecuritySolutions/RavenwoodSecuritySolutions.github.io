Client Agreement

Section 1 - Rules of Engagement

Before any tactical or otherwise combat related action is taken on behalf of Ravenwood Security Solutions*, members must ensure that they are within the boundaries specified herein.

Clients of Ravenwood Security Solutions are also expected to respect these rules within their ability, see the Client Agreement Section 1
Failure to do so may result in your access to our services being revoked.

*Exceptions are made at op-time for Lambda Core at the discretion of their Core Leader.

  • Members of Ravenwood Security Solutions may not fire upon unarmed bystanders, with the exception of those that still pose a tangible threat.
  • Members of Ravenwood Security Solutions may not fire upon armed bystanders unless initially fired upon.
  • Attacks of any kind upon external infrastructure not relating directly to Rec Room Custom Rooms is strictly prohibited.
  • Failure to abide by the rule above will result in your person or group being reported directly to the authorities relevant to both the victim of the attack and your geographic region, up to and including direct legal litigation.

Section 2 - Access Control

As an authorized accessor of the Ravenwood compound, you are obligated to maintain operational security during your operations on-post.
Primary obligations of this nature include, but are not limited to:

  • Maintaining at least one authorized member at the gate while an instance has at least one unauthorized user onsite.
  • Following the always-closed policy for all 4 initial gates.
  • Muting disruptive users and denying access to those without proper authorization.
  • If a breach does occur, terminating those involved and restoring operational security as soon as possible.

Section 3 - Core Leaders

If you are a Ravenwood Core Leader, you are obligated to upkeep the proficiency of your Core Members and bring on new ones at a consistent rate.
As a Core Leader, you are also expected to serve as a moderator of the Ravenwood Compound when necessary, settling disputes and granting/revoking access for users.

Failure to comply with proper operational security guidelines or repeated violations of the Rules of Engagement will result in your access being revoked, and an audit of all users in your Core.
